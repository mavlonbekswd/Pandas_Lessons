## Where to go after this course
The natural next steps for a job-ready analyst:
- **SQL** — the other half of analytics; maps almost 1:1 onto groupby/merge.
- **Statistics** — so your aggregations mean something.
- **Power BI / Tableau** — turn your cleaned data into dashboards.
- **Machine Learning** — scikit-learn; your cleaning/feature work feeds straight in.
- **Advanced Pandas & performance** (post-course extension): `categorical`,
  `eval`/`query`, chunking, Polars/DuckDB, Arrow-backed dtypes.
